### Lim, minimum, maximum

The minimum for π(x) is obtained at
```math
lim_{a \to - \infty} e^a / (1 + e^a) = 0,
```

and the maximum for π(x) is obtained at
```math
lim_{a \to \infty} e^a / (1 + e^a) = 1,
```
